# Mistake 5: Word Order in Embedded Questions

## Error

- Could you explain to me how can I use "supposed to"?
- Do you know where is the bank?
- Can you tell me what does this mean?

## Correct version:

- Could you explain to me how I can use "supposed to"?
- Could you explain how to use "supposed to"?
- Do you know where the bank is?
- Can you tell me what this means?
